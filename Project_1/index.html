Visualizing Time

Sketch 1

This sketch was the result of experimentation with fonts in processing. I wanted to create the most simple clock I could imagine, which was a wristwatch. It was pointed out to me that this is not in the orientation of a watch, but of a belt or necklace - I think it's a funny oversight and have elected to keep it this way.

Sketch 2

This is a static sketch made in javascript. I think the concept of time zones is super interesting because it's pretty arbitrary where exactly the line gets drawn for certain countries or states. I wanted to create a visualization that would show you the time in locations all over the world, and also the daylight at those locations.

Sketch 3

This sketch was also made in processing. The depiction of time here is that the top left triangle half is devoted to the hours, and the bottom right to the minutes. On every hour, two lines come from the corners and cross. Likewise, every minute, two lines cross on the bottom left.
